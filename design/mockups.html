How it works

No accounts. No server of ours. Just the chain.

Your WireGuard key is your identity. Payment is a fact on the Flux blockchain that every gateway reads independently. If our company vanished tomorrow, the deployed gateways keep running.

STEP 01

Generate & connect

The app makes a WireGuard keypair on your device and enrolls it at the nearest datacenter gateway. Free tier is throttled to 100 KB/s server-side.
